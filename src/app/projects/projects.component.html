Software Engineering

Software Engineering focuses on the design, development, and management of software. Students learn about programming, system design, and software development methodologies, preparing graduates to work in a variety of technology industries.

Agricultural Engineering

Agricultural Engineering focuses on applying engineering principles to solve problems in agriculture. Students study topics such as agricultural resource management, crop technology, and agricultural machinery.

Petroleum Engineering

Petroleum Engineering is oriented towards the study of exploration, extraction, and processing of petroleum and natural gas. Students learn about drilling techniques, petroleum refining, and energy resource management.
